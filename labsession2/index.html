Why I Admire Her

  • First woman to win a Nobel Prize, and the only person to win Nobel Prizes in two fields (Physics and Chemistry).
  • Her work led to the discovery of radium and polonium, which had major medical and scientific applications.
  • She made incredible sacrifices for science, often working in dangerous conditions without adequate safety measures, paving the way for future generations of female scientists.
